# Streaming Wars: The Value Benchmark

### The Goal
Does a cheaper subscription always mean better value? I analyzed the "Price-to-Content" ratio of major streaming services to find out.

### The Winning Insight
**Netflix** offers the highest volume of content per dollar (~419 titles/$1), making it the "value leader" despite having the highest monthly cost. **Disney+** has the lowest volume ratio in this group.
